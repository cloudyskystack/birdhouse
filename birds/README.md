### the birds' dynamics as a whole
everyone's usually pretty amicable towards each other. after all, when you live together, you have to learn how to coexist harmoniously, and the birds have had a good ten years or so to sort out any outstanding issues. it doesn't mean there are no conflicts, of course. living together is precisely the root cause of a lot of their problems with each other.  
morgan, as the only bird (so far) with a stable source of income, is the one who funds much of the birds' collective living expenses. thankfully, they don't have to spend much &mdash; most food can be foraged for, scarecrow scavenges for its mechanical scraps, and morgan forced delta to pay its own race registration fees.
