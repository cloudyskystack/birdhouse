## scarecrow
a maker, a tinkerer, but most of all, a loser. art fight page coming soon

### basic info
it’s not a crime to have two corvid characters…  
scarecrow is a crow who barely leaves its workshop. you’d think I’d do something more with the convenient irony of those first four words but you’re wrong. anyway scarecrow is usually there tinkering with all the loose parts it has scattered around.  
curoiusly, for all the time it spends in there, it’s only finished a handful of its own projects.
