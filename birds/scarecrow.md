## scarecrow
a maker, a tinkerer, but most of all, a loser. art fight page coming soon

### basic info
it’s not a crime to have two corvid characters…  
scarecrow is a crow who barely leaves its workshop. you’d think I’d do something more with the convenient irony of those first four words but you’re wrong. anyway scarecrow is usually there tinkering with all the loose parts it has scattered around. it does take commissions for custom projects sometimes, and although the quality is fairly good, they take a ridiculously long time to finish.  

### personality
scarecrow is a curious bird who likes to experiment and learn about all things mechanical! it doesn't apply the same principles to social interaction, though, and prefers to avoid any more of it than is necessary. except with morgan but only for discussing topics that interest the two of them specifically. scarecrow can be quite a bit prideful because it thinks it's so damn smart for dealing in STEM, but it also knows very well that its works are mostly nothingburgers and it still has a ways to go.  
they don't know it (since they don't talk much) but scarecrow and delta are a little similar in the way they fluff up when they're praised, yet they don't wholly believe it. a growth mindset is so crucial to both of their sort-of careers, but neither of them can fully grasp that &mdash; delta lacks direction, and scarecrow gets tunnel vision so easily.

### relationships
with [delta](https://cloudyskystack.github.io/birdhouse/birds/delta): delta is a bit too loud sometimes. but it also flies really fast, and scarecrow thinks it can harness that for something useful.  
with [morgan](https://cloudyskystack.github.io/birdhouse/birds/morgan): other than helping it carry its works to the post office, it’s nice having someone around to talk about the things scarecrow would usually like to talk about. it also admires morgan’s ability to just blend in with the rest of the cast. what, pff, no, scarecrow isn’t jealous of that.  

### fun facts!
scarecrow’s character was partly inspired by my role as scarecrow (wizard of oz) in a small production called _behind the curtain_! there’s many more interesting characters in there too that I might use for other (non-birdhouse) characters.
